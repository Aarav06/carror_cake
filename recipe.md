Preheat the oven to 180C/160C fan or gas 4.
Oil the base and sides of two 20cm cake tins with baking parchment.
Whisk the oil, yogurt, eggs, vanilla and zest in a jug.
Then, Mix the flour, sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg with a good pinch of salt in a bowl.
Next, squeeze any lumps of sugar through your fingers, while shaking the bowl a few times to bring the lumps up.
Combin both mixtures and add the carrots, raisins and half the nuts.
Mix Well.
Divide between the tins.
Bake for 20-25 mins or until when a skewer is inserted in the cake, comes out clean.
Leave the tins to cool.
Next, for the icing, beat the butter and sugar together until smooth.
Add the cheese slowly and beat again.
Remove the cakes from the tins and sandwich together with half the icing.
Top with the remaining icing and scatter with the remaining walnuts.
